Angry Dash

Angry Dash is a 2D game where your character has to cross a level strewn with obstacles.
With an easy grip, Angry Dash knows how to please any type of player, beginner or expert.
To prolong the pleasure, a level editor is present, you and your friends can have fun
on levels created by you or the community.

The Medieval Epic

The Medieval Epic is a 3D game, where your character evolves in a medieval universe, in search of a better future.
For this you will have to fight against various enemies,
from a simple prison guard, to a tyrannical king, to a bear.
The Medieval Epic aims to be a game of adventure, fighting, and strategy.

Windows
Android
